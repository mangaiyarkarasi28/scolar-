Common Special Characters

Here are some special characters and their HTML entities:

  • © - &copy; represents the copyright symbol
  • ® - &reg; represents the registered trademark symbol
  • ™ - &trade; represents the trademark symbol
  • < - &lt; represents less than symbol (<)
  • > - &gt; represents greater than symbol (>)
  • & - &amp;represents the ampersand symbol
